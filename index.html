The Life of Alexander the Great

  • 356 B.C. - Born in the capital of ancient Macedonia
  • 338 B.C. - Defeats the Sacred Band of Thebes at the Battle of Chaeronea
  • 336 B.C. - Becomes the king of Macedonia
  • 334 B.C. - Achieves victory against Darius III at the Battle of the Granicus River
  • 333 B.C. - Defeats Darius III once more at the Battle of Issus
  • 332 B.C. - Wins the siege of Tyre and attacks Gaza
  • 331 B.C. - Founds Alexandria and finds victory against Darius III at the Battle of Gaugamela
  • 330 B.C. - Sacks and burns Persepolis, the execution of Philotas, and the assassination of Parmenion
  • 329 B.C. - Crosses Hindu Kush and the Oxus river, then heads to Samarkand
  • 328 B.C. - Kills Black Cleitus
  • 327 B.C. - Gets married to Roxane and begins his trek to India
  • 326 B.C. - Achieves victory at the Battle of River Hydaspes
  • 324 B.C. - Mutiny at Opis and the death of Hephaestion
  • 323 B.C. - Dies at Babylon
